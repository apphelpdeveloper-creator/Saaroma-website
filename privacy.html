Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026
Saaroma is built on one principle — what you share here stays here. This policy explains exactly what we collect, what we do with it, and how you stay in control. We have written it in plain language so you do not have to decode legal jargon.
📦

What we collect

  • Your name or nickname — stored on your device only
  • Your phone number or email — used once to verify your identity, never stored after verification
  • Your journal entries and mood ratings — stored privately on your device using encrypted storage
  • Your chat conversations with Saaroma — stored on your device and sent to our AI service to generate responses
  • Your companion name, age range, and profession — stored on your device only
  • Your practice session activity — stored on your device only
⚙️

How we use your data

Your data is used only to make Saaroma work for you. Your journal entries and chat messages are sent to our AI service — powered by Anthropic Claude — to generate personalised responses. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond making your experience better.

We do not build a profile of you. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers.

☁️

Third party services

Saaroma uses Anthropic's Claude AI to generate companion responses. When you send a message, it is transmitted to Anthropic's servers to generate a reply. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing.

We send only what is needed — your message and a small amount of context. We do not send your name, phone number, or any identifying information to Anthropic.

Read Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

🛡️

You are in control

  • Delete your account at any time from the menu inside the app — this removes all your data from the device
  • Clear your chat history at any time from the chat screen
  • Your journal entries are stored only on your device — uninstalling the app removes them
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with any third party for advertising
  • Request deletion of any data at saaroma.ai/delete
🕐

How long we keep your data

Your data lives on your device for as long as you use the app. When you delete your account or uninstall the app, your data is removed from your device.

We do not keep copies on external servers beyond what is needed to generate your AI responses in real time. AI response data is not stored after the response is delivered.

🔒

Security

Your local data is stored using encrypted private storage on your device. It is not accessible to other apps and is not transmitted to any server except as described in this policy.

All communication between the app and our backend uses HTTPS encryption.

✉️

Questions about your privacy

If you have any questions about how Saaroma handles your data, or if you would like to make a data request, please contact us:

support@saaroma.ai

We will respond within 30 days.

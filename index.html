What Saaroma offers

Everything you need to feel heard

💬
Companion Chat
Talk to Saaroma like a warm, intelligent friend. It listens first, asks the right questions, and never rushes you toward answers.
📓
Private Journal
Write about your day, track your mood, and build a private record of your inner life. Auto-saves as you type.
🧘
Guided Practice
Four evidence-based sessions — one-minute reset, breathing pause, grounding, and one small action — to bring you back to yourself.
✦
Weekly Score
Every Sunday, Saaroma reads your week and delivers a personal reflection — what went well, what was heavy, and one focus for next week.
🔒
Completely Private
Your data lives on your device. Saaroma does not sell your information, does not show you ads, and does not build a profile of you.
🌍
Culturally Aware
Saaroma understands family pressure, career stress, and relationship dynamics across cultures — without you having to explain the context.
🌱
A companion that grows with you
Saaroma is not a quick fix — it is a practice. Like any meaningful relationship, it deepens over time. We recommend using Saaroma consistently for at least 6 months to experience its full benefit. Users who journal regularly and engage with the weekly reflection report the greatest sense of clarity and calm. The more you show up, the more Saaroma understands you.

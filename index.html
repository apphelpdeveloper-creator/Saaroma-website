What Saaroma offers

Everything you need to feel heard

💬
Companion Chat
Talk to Saaroma like a warm, intelligent friend. It listens first, asks the right questions, and never rushes you toward answers.
📓
Private Journal
Write about your day, track your mood, and build a private record of your inner life. Auto-saves as you type.
🧘
Guided Practice
Four evidence-based sessions — one-minute reset, breathing pause, grounding, and one small action — to bring you back to yourself.
✦
Weekly Score
Every Sunday, Saaroma reads your week and delivers a personal reflection — what went well, what was heavy, and one focus for next week.
🔒
Completely Private
Your data lives on your device. Saaroma does not sell your information, does not show you ads, and does not build a profile of you.
🌍
Culturally Aware
Saaroma understands the pressures of family expectations, career stress, and relationships across cultures — without you having to explain.
Our promise

Your private space should stay private

Saaroma is built on one principle — what you share here stays here.

  • Your journal and chat history live only on your device
  • We do not sell or share your personal data
  • No ads. No tracking. No profiling.
  • Delete your account and all data at any time
  • AI responses powered by Anthropic Claude — no personal data shared
🛡️
Private by default
Everything you write, say, or feel in Saaroma is stored privately on your device using encrypted storage. It is not meant to become a public profile of you.
